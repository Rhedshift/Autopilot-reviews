While the **Sony WH-CH720N** has an official list price of $149.99, it is so consistently discounted below $100 on Amazon and other major retailers that it represents one of the most compelling budget choices on the market. By bringing trickle-down technology from their legendary $350 flagship headphones into an ultra-lightweight chassis, Sony has created a comfort champion that is perfect for professional and daily casual use.

## Key Specifications

* **Driver Size:** 30mm dynamic drivers
* **Frequency Response:** 7Hz - 20kHz
* **Bluetooth Version:** 5.2 (Multipoint support)
* **Audio Codecs:** AAC, SBC
* **Battery Life:** Up to 35 hours (ANC On) / 50 hours (ANC Off)
* **Fast Charging:** 3-minute charge provides 1 hour of playback
* **Weight:** 192 grams (The lightest over-ear headphones in its class)

## Comfort & Build: 9.5/10
The standout feature of the WH-CH720N is its remarkable physical design.
* **The Weight:** At just **192 grams**, it is significantly lighter than most competitors. It feels practically weightless on your head.
* **Earcups & Padding:** The earcups are deep, spacious, and lined with high-quality pressure-relieving foam.
* **The Catch:** To achieve this weight, the build is almost entirely matte plastic. While it looks clean, it feels a bit hollow and does not fold down.

## Call Quality & Microphone: 9.5/10
If you need to make voice calls, record voice memos, or sit in virtual meetings, this is hands-down the best headset under $100.
* **Precise Voice Pickup:** Isolates your voice with incredible precision.
* **Wind Suppression:** Features a physical structure around the microphones designed to reduce wind noise.

## Active Noise Cancellation & Chipset: 8.7/10
Sony equipped the WH-CH720N with their proprietary **Integrated Processor V1**—the exact same chip used in their top-tier flagship models.
* **ANC Performance:** The V1 chip allows for highly natural active noise cancellation. It does an outstanding job at eliminating low-frequency drone.
* **Ambient Sound Mode:** Transparency mode sounds incredibly natural, with zero artificial metallic hiss or delay.

## Sound Quality: 8.2/10
Out of the box, the WH-CH720N has a relatively warm, consumer-friendly sound profile.
* **DSEE Upscaling:** Sony’s Digital Sound Enhancement Engine (DSEE) helps restore high-frequency details lost during Bluetooth compression.
* **App EQ:** Using the Sony Headphones Connect app, you can easily customize the 5-band EQ to your liking.

## Verdict & Recommendation
The **Sony WH-CH720N** is a triumph of design prioritization. By trading a luxury metal-and-leather build for a lightweight, ultra-comfortable plastic shell and packing it with Sony’s flagship V1 processor and superior mic arrays, they have delivered an unmatched productivity and communication powerhouse.
