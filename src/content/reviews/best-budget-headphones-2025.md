## Buying Guide: What to Look for in Budget ANC Headphones

When shopping for noise-cancelling headphones under $100, you have to accept a few compromises compared to $350 flagship models. Here is how to evaluate what matters most:

### 1. Active vs. Passive Noise Cancellation
* **Active Noise Cancellation (ANC):** Uses internal microphones to pick up ambient sound and generates inverse soundwaves to cancel it out. Under $100, ANC is excellent at removing *consistent, low-frequency noises*.
* **Passive Noise Isolation:** The physical barrier created by the earcups sealing against your head. A good, tight fit is crucial.

### 2. Battery Life and Fast Charging
Battery life has exploded in recent years. Do not settle for anything less than **30 hours with ANC turned on** in 2025.

### 3. Sound Quality and App Equalization
Most budget headphones are tuned to be "V-shaped" out of the box, meaning they have heavily boosted bass and elevated treble. Prioritize headphones that have a companion app with a customizable EQ.

### 4. Codecs: LDAC vs. AAC and SBC
* **SBC** is the standard, universal Bluetooth codec.
* **AAC** is optimized for Apple iOS devices.
* **LDAC** is Sony's high-resolution codec, supported on modern Android devices.
