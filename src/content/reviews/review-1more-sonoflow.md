Most budget headphones rely on a heavily bloated bass response to mask cheaper audio components. The **1MORE Sonoflow** takes the exact opposite approach. Boasting dual-dynamic drivers, custom-tuned sound profiles, and an astonishing 70-hour battery life, it delivers a clean, high-fidelity listening experience that stands completely unrivaled in its price tier.

## Key Specifications

* **Driver Size:** 40mm dynamic drivers with Diamond-Like Carbon (DLC) composite diaphragms
* **Frequency Response:** 20Hz - 40kHz (Hi-Res Audio and Hi-Res Wireless Certified)
* **Bluetooth Version:** 5.0 (Multipoint support)
* **Audio Codecs:** LDAC, AAC, SBC
* **Battery Life:** Up to 50 hours (ANC On) / 70 hours (ANC Off)
* **Fast Charging:** 5-minute charge provides 5 hours of playback
* **Weight:** 250 grams

## Sound Quality: 9.2/10
For a pair of headphones that regularly sells for around $65, the acoustic performance of the Sonoflow is nothing short of exceptional.
* **Balanced Tuning:** Tuned by four-time Grammy-winning sound engineer Luca Bignardi, the Sonoflow avoids the standard "muffled bass" trap of the budget market. The bass is punchy and tight, the mids are incredibly clear, and the treble is bright without being harsh.
* **Hi-Res LDAC Codec:** Supported on modern Android devices, LDAC allows you to stream audio at a significantly higher bitrate.

## Battery Life: 10/10
If there is one category where the Sonoflow completely embarrasses $400 premium headphones, it is battery endurance.
* **The Numbers:** You get **50 hours of continuous playback with ANC turned on**. Turning ANC off increases that figure to an incredible **70 hours**.
* **Real-World Impact:** For the average commuter listening for 2 hours a day, a single charge will easily last over a month.

## Design, Comfort & Accessories: 9.0/10
Despite its highly competitive price, 1MORE did not cut corners on build or accessories.
* **The Deluxe Case:** Unlike competitors that only include a cheap cloth pouch, the Sonoflow comes with a beautiful, custom-fit, hard-shell fabric travel case.
* **Comfort:** At 250 grams, they are extremely lightweight. The headband cushion and ear pads are plush, wrapped in soft protein leather.

## Active Noise Cancellation: 7.8/10
While the Sonoflow excels in sound and battery life, its ANC is good rather than groundbreaking.
* **How It Performs:** It utilizes 1MORE's proprietary **QuietMax Hybrid ANC** technology. It does a solid job of silencing low-frequency rumbles, such as train noise and airplane cabins.

## Verdict & Recommendation
The **1MORE Sonoflow** is a masterclass in budget engineering. By prioritizing premium acoustic design and exceptional battery capacity over hyper-aggressive noise-blocking algorithms, 1MORE has created the ultimate headphone for music lovers.
