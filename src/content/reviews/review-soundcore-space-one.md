The budget headphone space is fiercely competitive, but the **Anker Soundcore Space One** stands out as the ultimate heavyweight under $100. Combining modern aesthetics with active noise cancellation (ANC) that punches well above its class and a stellar companion app, it is the best all-rounder for most everyday listeners.

## Key Specifications

* **Driver Size:** 40mm dynamic drivers
* **Bluetooth Version:** 5.3 (Multipoint support)
* **Audio Codecs:** LDAC, AAC, SBC
* **Battery Life:** Up to 40 hours (ANC On) / 55 hours (ANC Off)
* **Fast Charging:** 5-minute charge via USB-C provides 4 hours of playback
* **Weight:** 265 grams

## Active Noise Cancellation: 8.5/10
Anker claims the Space One features a redesigned ANC system that is three times more effective at blocking out mid-range frequencies. In real-world testing, the ANC is remarkably impressive. 
* **Low-End Noise:** Constant low-frequency hums like city traffic and airplane engines are almost entirely neutralized.
* **Wind Reduction:** Anker’s wind-noise reduction algorithm works surprisingly well during outdoor walks.

## Sound Quality: 8.5/10 (With EQ Tuning)
Out of the box, the Space One is tuned with Soundcore's signature sound: a heavily boosted bass response and crisp, elevated treble. 
* **The Solution:** The free Soundcore companion app is where these headphones shine. By running the **HearID** hearing test or using the custom 8-band EQ, you can easily tone down the bass and open up the soundstage.

## Comfort & Design: 9.0/10
Anker has discarded the bulky plastic look of older models in favor of a sleek, modern aesthetic. The Space One features smooth matte plastic earcups mounted on a floating-axis headband.
* **Pads & Headband:** The memory foam ear cushions are clad in soft, protein leather.
* **Clamping Force:** It is well-balanced—firm enough to stay secure but gentle enough to avoid pressure points.

## Battery & Smart Features: 9.5/10
At **40 hours with ANC enabled**, the battery life is top-tier.
* **Wearing Detection:** An optical sensor automatically pauses your music when you take the headphones off.
* **Multipoint Connection:** Connects to two devices simultaneously.

## Verdict & Recommendation
The **Anker Soundcore Space One** sets a new benchmark for budget headphones. While it requires a quick trip to the Soundcore app to dial in the perfect sound signature, its premium features, excellent comfort, and highly effective active noise cancellation make it our undisputed **Best Overall Pick** under $100.
